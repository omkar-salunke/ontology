Proxy observationc back to ToC or Class ToC

IRI: http://linked.earth/ontology#ProxyObservation

is defined by
http://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S0277379113002011
The type of measurements made on the archives, whether chemical (e.g., Mg/Ca, d18O, TEX86, Sr/Ca....), physical (e.g. density, layer thickness) or biological (e.g. species abundance)
has sub-classes
Assemblage c, Chemical c, Physical obsrevation c, Spectral observation c
has members
Historic ni
