Introduction back to ToC

The instrument ontology defines the different types of instrument used to perform measurements in a paleo climate dataset. This ontology is the product of an evolving community effort, crowdsourced through the Linked Earth wiki.

The instrument ontology extends the Linked Earth Core Ontology.
