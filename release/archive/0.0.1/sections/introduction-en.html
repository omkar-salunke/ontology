Introduction back to ToC

The proxy archive ontology defines the different classes of archive types that are associated to the different observations in a paleo climate dataset. This ontology is the product of an evolving community effort, crowdsourced through the Linked Earth wiki.

The proxy archive ontology extends the Linked Earth Core Ontology .
